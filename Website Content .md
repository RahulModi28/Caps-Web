## About

**About CAPS**

**Who We Are**  
The Centre for Academic and Professional Support (CAPS) is a centre run by the students for the students, to bridge the gap between academia and industry. Through skill-sharing, collaborating with industry experts and facilitating upskillment sessions, the Centre helps students experience learning beyond the classroom. What sets CAPS apart is the peer training model, where a trained peer helps guide other students.

**Our Vision & Mission**  
**Vision**  
To be a benchmark in global academic circles by offering professional and academic support solutions through student-led leadership. 

**Mission**   
To provide a shared platform for students and faculty to exchange knowledge and experiences in order to develop academic excellence, professional competencies, and holistic growth**.** 

**Core Objectives**

* To make academic support student-driven and accessible: By offering peer-led training in both one-on-one and group formats, CAPS creates a supportive learning environment where students can strengthen academic foundations with confidence and ease.  
* To equip students with career-ready skills: Through structured sessions on communication, resumes, interviews, and industry engagement, CAPS empowers students to bridge the gap between academic learning and professional expectations.  
* To foster leadership through meaningful responsibility: As volunteers, students gain first-hand experience in planning, executing, and evaluating initiatives. They also develop critical leadership, teamwork, and project management skills in the process.  
* To promote holistic student development: Beyond academics and career preparation, CAPS encourages self-growth, collaboration, and reflective learning \- nurturing well-rounded individuals prepared to contribute meaningfully to any professional or academic space.

## Wings

**One on One Peer Training**  
The One-on-One Peer Training (OOPT) Wing is dedicated to fostering personalised and peer-driven learning within the university. Through individual training sessions, OOPT enables students to receive focused guidance on various professional, academic and life-skill modules from peer trainers. The wing aims to create a supportive learning environment where students can strengthen their skills, clarify concepts, and develop confidence through interactive and collaborative learning. By promoting knowledge-sharing and leadership among peers, OOPT contributes to the overall professional and personal development of the student community.

**Group Peer Training**  
The Group Peer Training (GPT) Wing under CAPS focuses on enhancing students’ academic, professional, and interpersonal skills through interactive classroom sessions. Peer trainers conduct sessions and workshops on topics such as communication, interview skills, presentation techniques, teamwork, and personal development, creating a comfortable and engaging learning environment where students can learn from fellow students. GPT aims to promote collaborative learning while helping both trainers and participants to increase their confidence in communication.

**Connect Wide**  
The Connect Wide wing of CAPS is dedicated to fostering meaningful connections between students, alumni, faculty and industry professionals through networking opportunities and various skill development initiatives. By organising panel discussions, workshops and industry interactions, this wing creates platforms for knowledge-sharing and professional growth. Connect Wide strengthens the university’s professional ecosystem by helping students gain valuable exposure to drive industry insights and career pathways. 

**Research & Assessment**  
The Research & Assessment Wing (R\&A) under CAPS cultivates an active research culture within the university. Through ethical interpretation and analysis of psychological assessments, the wing connects classroom learning with applied academic practice.  By utilising evidence-based psychometric tools and data driven insights, the wing helps students gain a deeper understanding of their personality, interest and developmental needs. R\&A’s aim is to foster self-awareness and holistic growth within the university community.

## **Committees**

**Media & PR**  
The Media and PR Committee is dedicated to visually documenting and showcasing the initiatives, events and impact of the Centre through visual storytelling. Capturing key moments, creating engaging visual content and maintaining media assets are its main role. The committee is the Centre's outreach, branding and engagement efforts. Through creative and technical expertise along with attention to detail, the committee helps preserve institutional memories and communicate CAPS’s activities effectively. 

**Learning & Development**  
The Learning and Development (L\&D) Committee creates meaningful and engaging learning experiences that enhance students' academic, professional, and personal growth. Through research-driven content, peer-led learning resources and educational material, the committee promotes continuous learning and skill development within the university community. By identifying emerging industry trends, designing accessible learning material, and fostering knowledge-sharing, the committee empowers students to remain adaptable, industry-ready, and committed to continuous learning.

**Operations & Analytics**

The Operations and Analytics Committee is dedicated to ensuring the efficient functioning, coordination, and improvement of the Centre's activities through data-driven decision-making and streamlined processes. By monitoring team and event performance, managing documentation and compliance requirements, maintaining organised systems, and developing standard operating procedures, the committee supports accountability, effectiveness, and operational excellence. Through strategic planning, analysis, and collaboration, the committee enables the Centre to optimise resources, enhance productivity, and deliver high-quality initiatives for the university community.

**Tech Tank**  
The Tech Tank Committee is dedicated to leveraging digital solutions to enhance the efficiency, accessibility, and impact of the Centre's operations and services. By developing and maintaining technological platforms like the CAPS website, supporting digital initiatives, and exploring innovative tools and automation solutions, the committee streamlines processes and improves user experience. Through technical support, continuous learning, and collaboration with various teams, Tech Tank contributes to the Centre's growth by fostering innovation, operational excellence, and digital transformation.
