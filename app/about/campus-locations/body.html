Our Campuses

CAPS operates across six Christ University campuses, making peer-led support accessible to students across Bangalore, Delhi NCR and Pune.

Main Campus

Central Campus, Bangalore. Fostering academic support and peer mentorship in the heart of the city.

Bannerghatta Road Campus

BRC, Bangalore. Cultivating critical thinking and writing skills among students of humanities and social sciences.

Kengeri Campus

Kengeri, Bangalore. Empowering engineering and management students with professional development sessions.

Yeshwanthpur Campus

BYC, Bangalore. Bringing student-led peer mentoring and career preparation workshops to a thriving new hub.

Delhi NCR Campus

Ghaziabad. Offering structured peer-to-peer learning and professional skills training in the national capital region.

Pune Lavasa Campus

Lavasa. Promoting self-guided modules, workshop schedules, and active student volunteering in the valley.
