The Four Pillars of CAPS

Every initiative CAPS runs is grounded in four core objectives — guiding how the Centre approaches academic support, career preparation, leadership development and holistic student growth.

Student-Driven Academic Support

By offering peer-led training in both one-on-one and group formats, CAPS creates a supportive learning environment where students can strengthen their academic foundations with confidence.

Career-Ready Skills

Through structured sessions on communication, resumes, interviews and industry engagement, CAPS empowers students to bridge the gap between academic learning and professional expectations.

Leadership Through Responsibility

As volunteers, students gain first-hand experience in planning, executing and evaluating initiatives — developing critical leadership, teamwork and project management skills in the process.

Holistic Student Development

Beyond academics and career preparation, CAPS encourages self-growth, collaboration and reflective learning — nurturing well-rounded individuals ready to contribute meaningfully to any professional space.
