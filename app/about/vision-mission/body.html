Our Vision & Mission

Where We're Headed

Our Vision

To be a benchmark in global academic circles by offering professional and academic support solutions through student-led leadership.

Why We Exist

Our Mission

To provide a shared platform for students and faculty to exchange knowledge and experiences in order to develop academic excellence, professional competencies, and holistic growth.

What Drives Us

Our vision and mission are grounded in three core beliefs that shape everything CAPS does.

Student-Led Leadership

CAPS is built on the belief that students are best placed to support other students — creating leadership through meaningful responsibility and lived experience.

Knowledge Exchange

A shared platform where students and faculty exchange knowledge and experiences, fostering academic excellence and professional growth across the university community.

Global Benchmark

Striving to set a standard that inspires other institutions — demonstrating what student-run academic and professional support can look like at scale.
