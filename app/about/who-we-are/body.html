About CAPS

The Centre for Academic and Professional Support (CAPS) is a centre run by the students for the students, bridging the gap between academia and industry. Through skill-sharing, collaboration with industry experts and upskillment sessions, CAPS helps students experience learning beyond the classroom.

The Peer Training Model

What sets CAPS apart is the peer training model — where a trained peer guides other students. This creates a relatable, comfortable and collaborative environment for growth.

Peer-Led Learning

Trained student peers deliver academic and professional support in a way that is relatable, practical and engaging for fellow students.

Industry Connections

Collaboration with industry experts and professionals bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world professional expectations.

Holistic Growth

Beyond academics, CAPS fosters self-growth, collaboration and leadership — nurturing well-rounded individuals ready for any professional space.
