The Voice of CAPS

Public Relations & Media Strategy

The Media & PR Committee is the voice of CAPS. We manage public relations, social media, photography, design, and news writing to ensure that the entire university community is informed of CAPS activities.

Responsibilities & Deliverables

Our members handle everything from graphic layout and design to writing, photography, and campaign coordination.

Content Creation

Writing blog articles, press releases, newsletters, and promotional copy for campus-wide distribution.

Visual Design

Creating premium flyers, brochures, banners, and digital assets following strict university branding guidelines.

Social Media

Managing Instagram, LinkedIn, and internal channels to drive student engagement and workshop attendance.

Photography & Video

Covering events, shooting student coordinator profiles, editing recap reels, and managing visual catalogs.
