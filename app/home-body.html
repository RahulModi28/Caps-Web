Grow with purpose. Lead change.

CAPS empowers student volunteers to develop their leadership identity. Peer mentors take charge of specialized divisions, designing training plans and supporting their fellow peers in a professional setting.

Volunteer Portal

Stage 1: Introductory Mentor

Student volunteers undergo introductory training to support coordinators, organize campaigns, and facilitate Writing Centre sessions under senior mentor supervision.

Stage 2: Active Peer Facilitator

Experienced volunteers conduct group workshops (GPT), analyze research formatting, lead specific wings, and mentor the new batch of junior volunteers.

Stage 3: Executive Committee

Student coordinators and team leads design strategic outreach initiatives, coordinate with university heads, and manage campus-wide operations for CAPS.
