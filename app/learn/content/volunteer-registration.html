Volunteering Levels

We provide a structured leadership growth path spanning three distinct tiers of responsibility.

Level 1: Volunteer

Support operations, coordinate event setups, maintain schedule files, shadow senior peer trainers, and assist with data logs.

Level 2: Coordinator

Conduct One on One and Group Peer Training sessions, design learning modules, and manage subpage content.

Level 3: Team Lead

Coordinate student committees, plan budgets, compile semester reports, oversee quality audits, and liaise with university heads.
